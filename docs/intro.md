# Introduction & Product Overview

**NeuroSense Africa** is a web-based educational and support platform designed specifically for neurodiverse children across Africa, with an initial launch focus in Nigeria. 

The platform supports children with **Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)**, **ADHD**, **speech differences**, and other **learning difficulties** by equipping their schools, classroom teachers, school counselors, and primary caregivers with actionable learning modules, progress dashboards, and support strategies.

---

## Dual-Tier Service Model

NeuroSense Africa runs on a hybrid model that maximizes accessibility for low-resource environments while offering advanced analytics and goal-directed custom personalization.

### Free Tier (Accessibility & Zero Friction)
- **Instant Access**: No registration, setup, or account creation is required to use foundational tools.
- **Offline Compatibility**: Cached library text and resources remain available without active internet connections.
- **Core Activities**:
  - **Speech Practice**: Listen & Repeat and Point & Say vocabulary play.
  - **Focus Skills (Basic)**: Matching, shape sequencing, and spot-the-difference exercises without dynamic scoring or timers.
  - **Social Skills Micro-Lessons**: Simple, culturally relevant lessons (emotions, greetings, turn-taking, sharing) paired with parent/teacher conversation prompts.

### Pro Tier (Structure & Measurable Outcomes)
- **Onboarding Personalization**: Warm, non-diagnostic profile wizard mapping targets, goals, and baseline observations.
- **Advanced Telemetry Tracking**: Automatically measures sound re-runs, response latency, choices, errors, and pacing logs.
- **Dashboard Analytics**: Handcrafted SVG metrics charts (Weekly Activity, Articulation Fluency) and engagement heatmaps.
- **Institutional Management (School Admin Console)**: Multi-tenant dashboards allowing school directors to manage teachers, child assignments, and billing.
- **Report Generation**: Print-ready PDF report downloads optimized for parent or therapist handovers.
- **Weekly Emails**: Automated summary digests dispatched weekly via Resend.

## User Personas

| Persona | Tier | Primary Actions |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **School Administrator** | Pro | Manages institution roster, adds staff, maps student assignments, manages Stripe billing. |
| **Classroom Teacher** | Free + Pro | Uses educational libraries for awareness, guides assigned students through focus and speech games. |
| **Parent / Caregiver** | Free + Pro | Completes onboarding, reviews child metrics, practices speech sounds at home. |
| **School Counselor** | Free + Pro | Reviews progress charts, exports PDF reports for therapists, edits student goals. |
| **Child (4 - 14 Years)** | Free + Pro | Practices interactive lessons under adult supervision. |

---

## Project Objectives

1. **Accessibility First**: Remove friction for classroom teachers and families through instant-load, zero-signup basic games.
2. **Measurable Outcomes**: Provide parents and educators with objective insights and suggested strategies based on real gameplay data.
3. **Data Security**: Securely isolate student records to prevent cross-tenant data leakage.
4. **Clinical Safety**: Framed purely as a supportive educational resource—**strictly out-of-scope** for formal diagnostic testing or clinical assessments.
